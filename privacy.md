### Introduction

This privacy policy explains how this website handles visitor information. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to transparency about our data practices.

### Information We Collect

#### Automatically Collected Information

When you visit this website, the following information may be automatically collected:
- Browser type and version
- Operating system
- Pages visited and time spent on pages
- Referring website addresses
- IP address (anonymized)

#### Information You Provide

We do not actively collect personal information unless you:
- Submit a comment (if commenting is enabled)
- Contact us via email
- Subscribe to our newsletter (if available)

### How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- Improve website performance and user experience
- Analyze website traffic and usage patterns
- Respond to your inquiries
- Send updates (only if you subscribe)

### Cookies and Local Storage

This website uses:
- **LocalStorage** to save your dark mode preference
- **Essential cookies** for basic website functionality

We do not use third-party advertising cookies or tracking cookies.

### Third-Party Services

This website may use the following third-party services:

#### GitHub Pages
This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect visitor information as described in their [Privacy Statement](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-privacy-statement).

#### Analytics (if enabled)
If analytics are enabled, we use privacy-focused analytics that do not collect personal information or use cookies. All data is anonymized and aggregated.

#### Comments (if enabled)
If you post comments through our commenting system:
- Your comment content and name (if provided) will be publicly visible
- We store this information to display your comment
- You can request deletion of your comments by contacting us

### Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

### Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access any personal information we hold about you
- Request correction of inaccurate information
- Request deletion of your information
- Object to processing of your information
- Withdraw consent at any time

### External Links

This website may contain links to external sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of other websites.

### Children's Privacy

This website is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

### Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
